# Support

**Medication Tracker**

## Getting Help

If you're experiencing an issue or have a question about Medication Tracker, here's how to get help:

### Report a Bug or Request a Feature

Please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/damianrickard/MedicationTracker/issues).

When reporting a bug, please include:
- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Your device model and iOS/macOS version

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Where is my data stored?**
All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

**Will I lose my data if I delete the app?**
Yes. Since all data is stored on your device, deleting the app will remove all medication data. Consider noting your medications before deleting.

**How do notifications work?**
Medication Tracker schedules local notifications on your device to remind you when a medication is due. Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings > Medication Tracker > Notifications.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes. Medication Tracker works entirely offline with no internet connection required.
